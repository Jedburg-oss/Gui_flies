METHODOLOGY

How this works.

DATA SOURCE
KAYAK.com
Direct headless scraping via Playwright + Chromium. Each price reflects the cheapest itinerary KAYAK displayed at scrape time.
CADENCE
Every 2 weeks
Each scrape produces one CSV file. Files are stored in data/ and tracked in data/manifest.json.
COVERAGE
4 airports
Tokyo Narita (NRT), Tokyo Haneda (HND), Osaka Kansai (KIX), Nagoya Chubu (NGO). Trip length fixed at 14 days.
LIMITATIONS
Snapshots
Prices reflect the moment of scraping — not real-time fares. Always verify current price on the airline site before booking.
